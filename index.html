Company Profile

About BONAFIDE SAFARI LIMITED

BONAFIDE SAFARI LIMITED is a proudly Tanzanian-owned and operated tourism company. Established with a deep passion for our country's natural beauty and rich cultural heritage, we are committed to providing unparalleled travel experiences while promoting sustainable tourism practices. Our local roots mean we have an intimate understanding of the destinations, the wildlife, and the communities, allowing us to offer truly authentic and enriching journeys. We believe in empowering local talent and contributing directly to the welfare of our people and conservation efforts.

Our mission is to connect travelers with the heart of Tanzania, offering a diverse range of safaris, treks, beach holidays, and cultural immersions. We prioritize personalized service, safety, and creating memories that last a lifetime.
